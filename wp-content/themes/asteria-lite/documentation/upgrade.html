Why PRO?

Unlimite Style

With Asteria PRO you can have versetile look by just setting the style options form the theme options page. You can change the color od any elements you want.

Unlimite Fonts

In Asteria PRO you can select fonts from 500+ fonts for your site, just from the theme options page.

3 Sliders

In Asteria PRO you can choose from any of the 3 sliders as your frontpage slider. You can also also use shortcodes to display sliders in your posts, pages and text widgets.

Custom Backgrounds

With PRO you can choose different background for different pages to give your site a unique look.

Custom Page Header

With PRO you can set different page header images for different pages.

Video Thumbanails

With the PRO version you can also set video thumbnails for your frontpage posts, where you can watch the video on frontpage withou going to the post.

6 Posts Layouts

In PRO You can display your posts with any of the 6 layouts.

29 Shortcodes

Asteria PRO comes with 29 unique shortcodes to enrich your content.

15 Widgets

Asteria PRO comes with 15 easy to use and essential widgets that you can use in your widget areas from Appearance> Widgets.

6 Frontpage Elements

In PRO You can display 6 frontpage elements where you can only display 3 fronpage elements in the Lite version.

Logo Upload

In Asteria PRO you can add your Logo in the header of your site.

Premium Support

The PRO version users get priority support through emails and forums.
